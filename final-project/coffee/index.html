Ninth Street Espresso

Ninth Street Espresso is my favorite coffee shop in Alphabet City. They consistently produce delicious coffee in a relaxed and comfortable space. I usually buy their Alphabet City blend beans for brewing at home on those cold mornings.

They have two locations in the area, one at 9th and C, and one at 10th and B. The Avenue B location has art on the walls for sale by local artists. Both are great places to get work done but beware: outlets are scarce. Come fully charged!

Order this: Latte and an almond croissant
