Ninth Street Espresso

Ninth Street Espresso is my favorite coffee shop in Alphabet City. They consistently produce delicious coffee in a relaxed and chill space with only the tiniest sliver of hipster pretention.

They have two locations in the area, one at 9th and C, and one at 10th and B. This may seem silly but I find myself going to both equally depending on the day. The Avenue B location also has art on the walls for sale by local artists.

Order this: Latte and an almond croissant
