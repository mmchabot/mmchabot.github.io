The Wayland

Something happens when you reach your thirties. It's a lot harder to get your friends to come to a slightly out of the way neighborhood that is more than a 10 minute walk from the subway. Luckily, I always have The Wayland in my back pocket(and backyard). Great cocktails and small plates in an inviting atmosphere that's perfect for catching up with friends but intimate enough for a date night. Their weekend brunch is solid too but always a long wait.

My favorite cocktail is the Smoked and Roasted, which has mezcal, fresh pineapple, lemon, and chipotle-infused agave nectar with chili salt on the rim. It's the perfect mix of smoky, spicy and sweet.

Date night order: Smoked and Roasted, Garden Variety Margarita, Sausage Bread with Ricotta
